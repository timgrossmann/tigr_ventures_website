The Model

Joint ventures, not vendor relationships.

Tigr Ventures partners with domain experts to build AI-powered products. You bring deep knowledge of your field. We bring the technical execution to turn that knowledge into software. We co-own what we build.

No dev teams to manage. No six-month discovery phases. No bloated SOWs. One operator, fully AI-native, building with you as a co-founder, not for you as a client.

Skin in the game. On both sides.

How It Works

From conversation to co-ownership.

Every partnership starts the same way: understanding whether the fit is right.

01

Evaluate the Fit

We assess whether your domain expertise and our AI execution can produce something neither could alone. Not every idea is a fit. That honesty is the starting point.

02

Define the Product

Together we scope a specific, buildable product. What does it do, who uses it, and what is the business model. No ambiguity.

03

Build and Iterate

We build fast, starting with a working MVP. You validate with real domain knowledge. We iterate in tight cycles until the product works.

04

Launch and Own

The product goes to market. Revenue is shared. You own what we built together. No licensing traps, no dependency on us to keep the lights on.
